That's a low-level error, Linux can't even detect your device properly. All 
attempts to communicate with it result in an error. This can indicate a broken 
device, faulty cabling, a broken USB host controller or a low-level bug in the 
USB stack. The uvcvideo driver has nothing to do with this, as it doesn't even 
get called.
