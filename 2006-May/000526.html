When I say "capture" I just mean streaming video, usually to a display. I  
haven't tried writing to AVI or MPG files but that part is quite unrelated  
to V4L2 or the UVC driver itself, so I can't help you a lot with that part.

GStreamer has an avimux plugin, so with the right pipeline you should be  
able to write webcam streams into AVI files. If you just want to display  
the video on the screen, try something like:
	gst-launch v4l2src ! xvimagesink

I'm using the CVS version of GStreamer 0.10 here.
