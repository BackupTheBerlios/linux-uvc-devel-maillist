Hi all, 

just writing in to report some big improvements I've noticed.

My setup:
Logitech Quickcam Fusion
Kernel 2.6.15.3 (from kernel.org)
uvcvideo revision 34
Kopete 0.12 beta 2

config window, then closed it, and opened it again kopete would crash or the
