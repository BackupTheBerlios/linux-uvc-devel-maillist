I expected that. Still I wanted to ask. YUYV is nicer for processing
(object recognition) than Bayer, but has double the data rate.

Of course for 1.3 Mpix cameras running at 640x480 YUYV makes more sense,
because there it carries non-redundant information. Not so with VGA
cameras.

Which makes me wonder: How does the image quality of the Fusion/Orbit MP
compare to the Pro 5000?
