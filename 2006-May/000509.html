On Wed, 2006-05-24 at 16:29 +0200, Vojtech Pavlik wrote:

> So the question is: Is it possible to get the raw data out of the
> QuickCam Pro 5000 CCD VGA sensor?
> 

Getting a raw Bayer image is strangely not part of the UVC spec, and is
thus not supported on the Quickcam Pro 5000 as best as I can tell.

It sounds like you may have used an undocumented feature to trick your
iBot into giving raw output, and it's possible there's a similar hack
for the Quickcam.  In the end, it may not save bandwidth however because
the Quickcam Pro 5000 seems to always allocate more isochronous
bandwidth than it really needs.  I suppose this is an attempt to reduce
per-frame latency.
