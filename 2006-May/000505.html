Hi Geoffrey,

> I'd like to know if the quickcam pro 4000 will be supported.

The Quickcam Pro 4000 is an old generation webcam. It's not compatible with 
the USB Video Class standard, and will thus not be supported by the linux-uvc 
driver.

There is, however, a Linux driver already available for the webcam. A quick 
search on Google with the "QuickCam Pro 4000 Linux" keywords should be enough 
to help you.
