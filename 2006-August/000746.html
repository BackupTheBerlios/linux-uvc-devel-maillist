Yes, they exist but they will never be used by the UVC driver. AFAIK they  
are only used by TV cards and similar devices but in the case of a USB  
webcam, audio and video are two completely separate functions.

http://linuxtv.org/downloads/video4linux/API/V4L2_API/spec/x273.htm

Why would you want V4L2 to know about webcam audio anyway? In an  
application the user should be able to pick the video and audio input  
independently. Maybe someone wants to use a webcam with their headset.
