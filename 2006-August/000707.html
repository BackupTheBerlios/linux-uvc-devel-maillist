On Fri, 2006-08-11 at 18:42 +0800, Ajay Kumar Mishra wrote:
> 2. Can the stream capture be parallelized for the three devices such
> that they seem to capture the scene all at once? 

You could run three instances of uvccapture from the same script at
roughly the same time by appending an & at the end of each line. They
still won't run at *exactly* the same instance due to variable
initialisation times for each camera and other environmental factors but
it'll be closer than capturing from the three cameras sequentially.

eg:

uvccapture -d /dev/video0 &
uvccapture -d /dev/video1 &
uvccapture -d /dev/video2 &
