On Tue, 29 Aug 2006 13:52:57 -0700, Iain Lennon <ril at doctors.org.uk> wrote:

> After some searching on mailing lists I wonder whether the USB webcam  
> embedded in my HP DV1680ea is a UVC compatible one

That should be easy to find out. You have the UVC compatible one if:
- On Linux: it is recognized by the Linux UVC driver (check the syslog)
- On Windows: it runs with the Microsoft UVC driver (check the device  
manager)

> I don't know which flavor is in my laptop, but wonder if you could add  
> support for it to your driver

That's the beauty of UVC: The driver doesn't have to know about compatible  
devices explicitly. The devices are self-descripting, so if the USB device  
descriptor contains the right values, the UVC driver can handle the camera  
in a generic manner.
