Ajay Mishra wrote:
> 1. I need to grab the frames from the webcam at regular interval 
> specified by me. And it is not provided in "luvcview". Can you please 
> suggest the solution?

uvccapture was written for just such a requirement, it grabs a single
frame from the webcam at a specified interval, it can be found at
http://staticwave.ca/source/uvccapture/, it requires libjpeg in order to
function.

> 2. When I try to use -g option with luvcview, I get the following error
> 
> /"[g0500124 at nusnet-50-165 ~]$ luvcview -d /dev/video0 -g -f jpg -s 640x480
> uvcview verion 0.1.6
>  size width: 640 height: 480
> Video driver: x11
> A window manager is available
> video /dev/video0
> /dev/video0 does not support read i/o
> 
>  Init v4L2 failed !! exit fatal"

Not sure what the -g option is supposed to do in luvcview, but I believe
the "read" method of capturing frames is not yet supported in the uvc
driver, only the mmap method.

Hope this sorts things out.
