The Logitech C210 works fine on my Ubuntu GNU/Linux 2.6.31 32-bit system.

from dmesg:
[266285.384615] usb 1-5.2: configuration #1 chosen from 1 choice
[266285.385567] uvcvideo: Found UVC 1.00 device <unnamed> (046d:0819)
[266285.486162] input: UVC Camera (046d:0819) as /devices/pci0000:00/0000:00:1d.7/usb1/1-5/1-5.2/1-5.2:1.0/input/input32

guvc shows it as offering 640x480 at up to 30fps in both MJPG and YUYV formats.  The video is pretty good for such an affordable camera.  I have
a few notes on the focus ring and infrared filter at my website:
