I bought this nice looking webcam for only $13.68 with free shipping
included on dealextreme.

 http://www.dealextreme.com/details.dx/sku.12939~r.28238939


At first I couldn't get it to work. It gave the errors :

Failed to query (135)
and
Failed to query (130)

But I got it to work by setting quirks=2

Now it works. Tested with VLC and Ekiga.

With luvcview it still gives a black window or an all green one with
buttons at the bottom.
