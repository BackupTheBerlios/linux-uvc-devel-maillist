I have just done a bit more testing.
It seems that if the uvcvideo driver gets loaded when when the camera is
 NOT plugged in, then the /dev/video0 never gets created.

I would suggest that in my case at least, during boot, the uvcvideo gets
loaded before the USB controllers have had time to recognise that the
camera is there.

I found this because I accidentally left the camera out when I did a
reload and the xawtv -hwscan showed that the /dev/video was not there.
Even after plugged the camera in it still would not come up.
Another reload fixed it.

I could probably prove this if I knew how to delay the loading of
uvcvideo until the end of the boot sequence?
