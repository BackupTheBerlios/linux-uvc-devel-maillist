> I would like to know if USB1 is supported with the UVC driver, on any
> webcam.
> If not, how can I choose a cam which works with USB1?
>
> (I am specifically looking for a USB1 webcam which supports infrared)

The uvcvideo driver doesn't depend on a specific USB version. As far as I 
know, pretty much all consumer UVC webcams support both full speed (USB 1.1)

and high speed (USB 2.0). Obviously, you won't be able to stream
uncompressed 
video data at high resolutions using full speed, so you should pick a webcam

with MJPEG support.
