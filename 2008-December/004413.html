It might, and it might not. The uvcvideo driver supports UVC compliant cameras 
only. Could you please check http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/faq/ for 
instructions to see if your camera if UVC compliant ?

If the camera is UVC compliant, please report the full USB descriptors using

lsusb -d 06f8:3003 -v

with usbutils 0.72 or newer (0.73+ preferred).
