If I understand you correctly, you have an application that streams video from 
a UVC device, and you want to stop that video stream (or fade it to black) 
from anothere application. The uvcvideo driver can't do that. This kind of 
policy must be implemented in userspace by making the applications communicate 
with eachother.

> I've tried to "rmmod -f" the module without success :-(

kill -9 the application using the webcam, the effect will be similar :-)
