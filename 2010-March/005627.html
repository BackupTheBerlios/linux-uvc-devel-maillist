Jeff, can you try setting the quirks parameter to 16.
change uvcvideo.conf in /etc/modprobe.d to
options uvcvideo quirks=16 trace=255

sudo rmmod uvcvideo
sudo modprobe uvcvideo

Try luvcview and check dmesg.
