Hi,

I want to report this webcam as supported by the uvc driver under
Linux 2.6.31.5 on x86_64 (libc 2.9; openSUSE 11.1)


What doesn't look right is this:

  $ cat /dev/video0
  cat: /dev/video0: No such device

although that device node works for video software.



GO:

(SVN-r29799-4.3) and kdenlive. There are several error messages like the
following, but they don't seem to matter:

  ioctl: VIDIOC_S_STD(std=0x0 []): Invalid argument



NO-GO:
